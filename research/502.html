Yaw Osei Adjei 502

On this page, I describe all my academic failures (at least the big ones I remember). Some failures are random, and others are justified. Some are not actually failures, but they often seem so at the moment, but they all suck.

The purpose of this is to showcase that behind any shiny success --- whether it's a paper, a scholarship, or a job, many failures hide behind it. We don't typically discuss them publicly, but it's important to anticipate, acknowledge, and learn how to deal with them.

By making this page public, I hope to help normalize failure and help others (including myself), understand that failure is part of the process and more common than it appears.
